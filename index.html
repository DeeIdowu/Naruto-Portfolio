About Me:

My name is Dennis Idowu I am a Full Stack Developer. I began coding at a mere age of 12 in middle school dabbling and learning the intricacies of HTML and CSS via use of a web hosting site called FreeWebs. I took a hiatus from the trade as I was compelled to focus on sports, I returned and rekindled my old flame/passion for technology, upon the reading of Robert Greene's book "Mastery". Often we are a product of our childhood tendencies and influences - this is pertaining to hobbies we engage in which evidently can suffice as a purpose and skill we should engage in as a career, so I made the decision to enroll myself into a Coding BootCamp by Trilogy hosted by the University of Toronto in 2018, under their tutelage I was enable to bolster my prior knowledge of Web and Application Development via learning the MERN stack. (MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, and NodeJS) on top of the HTML, CSS and JavaScript within a hasty and vigorous 6 months.
